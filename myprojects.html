Hackathon Challenge - Learn to Read Tablet App

The problem: Each year 640,000 babies are born in the UK. Worldwide it's 140m meaning every year, that's how many children start the process of learning to read.ðŸ“š Anyone who has ever taught anyone to learn to read will understand the challenge of starting the literacy journey!

The coding challenge: The teams were tasked with creating a web app to make learning to read interactive and fun. They could take any technical approach they wanted and had to present their creative solution to the judges by 5pm that day.

We won! We made a Jolly Phonics app (Intended for tablets and Ipads) that helps toddlers pair phonic sounds with letters and then progress to full words.
